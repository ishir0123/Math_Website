MathJax LaTeX Example

Here is an example of LaTeX formatted text: \( \int_0^1 x^2 dx = \frac{1}{3} \)

And here is a more complex example: \(R_{1} \Longleftrightarrow R_{1}\)

You can also display matrices: \( \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{bmatrix} \)

And even use Greek letters: \( \alpha, \beta, \gamma \)
